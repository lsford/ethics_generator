Exploring the Ethical Choices of Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to the Ethics Generator

The purpose of this platform is to collect information on how society thinks artificially intelligent machines, such as autonamous vehicles and health information technology, should act when faced with a moral dilemma.

We hope that the information collected can benefit researchers, policy makers and manufactures, as artificial intelligence progresses beyond science fiction into our everyday lives.

The buttons below will present you with multiple scenarios, focusing on autonamous vehicles or healthcare information technology. As a impartial spectator, you are asked to judge each scenario, and decide which outcome you would prefer.

Please be aware that the scenarios are not a true reflection of real life, and in some situations, may present a more black and white scenario than would actually be the case.
