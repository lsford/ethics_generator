Healthcare Information Technology

Here you will be presented with a series of scenarios about the use of artificial intelligence in the healthcare industry. Each scenario will include a visual and written description of a situation, and in some cases the situations outcome.

For each scenario please select the situation you find preferable. There is an example scenario below, please press start when you are ready to begin. No knowledge of the topic is required to answer the questions.

Example Scenario

Your local gp surgery uses an artificial intelligence to check the symptoms of patients when they book an appointment. The artificial intelligence analyses the patients symptoms and identifies possible illnesses the individual could be suffering from. You use the symptom checker as you have recently been suffering from flu-like symptoms for the last 7 days.
